Bubble Pillar Buttons Explanation

Control Buttons

On/Off: Turns the system on or off.

Sleep: Starts the sleep timer. Bubble pillar will shut down in 30 seconds unless you turn off the system or press the Cancel Sleep button. The timer that appears to the left of sleep button indicates when Centerpieces will turn off.

Lights Buttons

Fade: Bubble pillar lights switch to fade mode where both top and bottom lights will have the same color. The lights will continually fade from one color to another in a predetermined manner.

Fade Split: Bubble pillar lights switch to fade split mode where the top and bottom lights will have different colors. Top and bottom lights will continually fade from one color to another in a predetermined manner.

Custom Color: You can choose your desired color from a color wheel and Bubble pillar will set both top and bottom lights to your desired color. NOTE: You must either let go of the color wheel or hold your finger at the same color for a short period of time in order to send the command to bubble pillar to change to your desired color.

Mix Mode: Periodically switches between fade and fade split lights modes.

Bubble Hub Connection Button

Some of the other Bubble products(bubble wall for example) will send radio signals to all other Bubble products in order to make controlling of multiple Bubble products easier.Press the Disconnect From Bubble Hub button if you want to prevent some particular Bubble Pillar from listening to radio transmissions from other Bubble products.
