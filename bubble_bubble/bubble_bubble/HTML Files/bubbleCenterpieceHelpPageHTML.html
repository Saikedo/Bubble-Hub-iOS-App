Bubble Centerpiece Buttons Explanation

Control Buttons

On/Off: Turns the system on or off.

Sleep: Starts the sleep timer. Centerpieces will shut down in 30 seconds unless you turn off the system or press the Cancel Sleep button. The timer that appears to the left of sleep button indicates when Centerpieces will turn off.

Lights Buttons

Fade: Centerpieces lights switch to fade mode. The lights will continually fade from one color to another in a predetermined manner.

Custom Color: You can choose your desired color from a color wheel and the centerpieces will set the lights to your desired color. NOTE: You must either let go of the color wheel or hold your finger at the same color for a short period of time in order to send the command to the centerpieces and change the lights to your desired color.

Brightness Slider

With brightness slider, you can control the brightness of the centerpieces. Setting the brightness slider to 0 will keep the Centerpieces bubbles running but will turn off the lights. NOTE: You must either let go of the brightness slider or hold your finger at the same brightness level for a short period of time in order to send the command to centerpieces to change to your desired brightness.
