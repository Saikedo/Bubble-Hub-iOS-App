Bubble Wall Buttons Explanation

Control Buttons

On/Off: Turns the system on or off.

Sleep: Starts the sleep timer. Bubble wall will shut down in 30 seconds unless you turn off the system or press the Cancel Sleep button. The timer that appears to the left of sleep button indicates when Centerpieces will turn off.

Bubble Buttons

Small Bubbles: Use only small bubbles

Big Bubbles: Use only big bubbles

Small + Big: Use small and big bubbles simultaneously.

Pyramid Bubble: Produce various shapes by using a combination of small and big bubbles.

Pillar Bubble: Switches bubble mode to produce various shapes by using a combination of small and big bubbles. NOTE: You might hear the water overflowing into the water reservoir depending on the height of the water inside the panel. This is a normal behavior as long as the alarm is not turning on or you are not seeing any water leakage.

Mix Mode: Periodically switches through all bubble modes.

Lights Buttons

Rainbow 1: Bubble Wall lights switch to rainbow 1 mode.

Rainbow 2: Bubble Wall lights switch to rainbow 2 mode.

Fade: Bubble Wall lights switch to fade mode. The lights will continually fade from one color to another in a predetermined manner.

Fade Split: Bubble Wall lights switch to fade split mode, where top and bottom lights will have different colors. The lights will continually fade from one color to another in a predetermined manner..

Custom Color: You can choose your desired color from the color wheel and Bubble Wall will set the lights to your desired color. NOTE: You must either let go of the color wheel or hold your finger at the same color for a short period of time in order to send the command to Bubble Wall to change to your desired color.

Mix Mode: Periodically switches through all the lights modes.

Audio Control Buttons

Audio controls should mostly be self-explanatory with one exception. If you choose the USB mode, SD Card Mode or AUX mode without having USB, SD Card or Aux cable connected to the speaker, the speaker might switch to an FM radio mode. You can either provide the necessary connections for the mode you want to use or switch to Bluetooth mode where you can pair your phone with the speaker and stream your desired content to the speaker.
